Polish Farmer Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 Polish Sausage (Kielbasa) Sliced
  • 2 Yellow Onions wedged
  • 3 Granny Smith Apples sliced
  • 1 Head of Cabbage wedged
  • 1 cup Apple Juice
  • 1 lb Green Beans
  • Yellow Potatoes wedged

Instructions

  1. In a large electic skillet combine all the ingredients except the apples.
  2. Add apple juice, stir, then cover and let simmer.
  3. Once ingredients are almost to desired consistency, add apples, cover and simmer until apples cook down

Christmas Eve Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Salmon Fillet
  • 2-3 Lemons (save slice for garnish)
  • 1 Stock Rosemary (2nd stock for garnish)
  • 2-3 Cloves Garlic
  • 3 Sweet Onions
  • 1 Jar Capers (drain and rinse)
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • Basil and Parsley
  • Olive Oil (use slightly less amount than butter)

Instructions

  1. Cook onions in olive oil and butter slowly so that they carmelize. Add a little salt
  2. Mix in rosemary, basil, parsley, fresh squeezed lemon juice, and garlic
  3. Put salmon in casserole dish. Pour mixture over salmon, making sure th salmon is covered in onions to prevent the fish from drying out when it bakes.
  4. Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Add capers midway through baking to warm them. Make sure fish flakes with fork
