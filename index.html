Polish Farmer Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 Polish Sausage (Kielbasa) Sliced
  • 2 Yellow Onions wedged
  • 3 Granny Smith Apples sliced
  • 1 Head of Cabbage wedged
  • 1 cup Apple Juice
  • 1 lb Green Beans
  • Yellow Potatoes wedged

Instructions

  1. In a large electic skillet combine all the ingredients except the apples.
  2. Add apple juice, stir, then cover and let simmer.
  3. Once ingredients are almost to desired consistency, add apples, cover and simmer until apples cook down
